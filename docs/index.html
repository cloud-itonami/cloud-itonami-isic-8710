Residential nursing care facilities

An open-source business blueprint for independent nursing homes and community long-term-care operators. Provides resident intake, care-plan assessment, medication administration and incident-response finalization with an independent governance layer and immutable audit ledger. Licensed staff retain full control over high-stakes decisions; the system enforces regulatory compliance and evidence requirements without replacing clinical judgment.

Core capabilities:
  • Resident intake and jurisdiction-specific regulatory assessment
  • Care-plan proposal with evidence-based recommendations
  • Medication administration and incident-response finalization (human-approved)
  • Immutable audit ledger for every decision and action
